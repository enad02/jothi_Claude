A system that keeps improving

Every cohort teaches us something. Every change shows up in the next cohort’s grades.

In 2022, our students told us what they wanted more of: exam-style questions, tighter alignment to the specification, stronger correction routines, clearer feedback loops. We listened. We overhauled the system to focus more on mock tests for GCSE students. We built monthly mastery tests with an 80% pass mark. We made past-paper rehearsal a standard weekly rhythm.

The next GCSE cohort’s Grade 7+ rate rose from 52% to 76%.

That’s not luck. That’s what happens when a tutoring business treats its own results as evidence — not marketing.
