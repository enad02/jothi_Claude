Tutor Quality

Tutors are trained before they teach independently.

Jothi tutors are not matched casually with students. We look for subject understanding, clear explanation, care for student progress, and the ability to work inside a structured teaching model.

New tutors go through screening, teaching assessment, safeguarding training, and mentored development before they take full teaching responsibility.

This protects the consistency parents expect from Jothi: clear teaching, responsible support, and better visibility over progress.

What we look for

  • passion for tutoring and student progress
  • ability to meet students at their current level
  • willingness to learn and keep improving
  • strong subject understanding
  • confidence using teaching technology

Interested in teaching with Jothi?

We welcome tutors who care about students, communicate clearly, and are willing to learn the Jothi teaching model.
