## Current business summary

Jothi Learning is a mid-market-to-premium online tutoring business for students in Years 7-13, with a strong emphasis on Maths, GCSE Science, and A-Level Maths.

Core public positioning:
- Warm, nurturing, premium, and serious
- Structured teaching rather than patchy tutoring
- Visible progress through teaching, testing, tracking, parent updates, and pathway guidance
- WhatsApp-led parent communication

## Current programme model

The public model is monthly programme-cycle pricing.

Standard programmes are live small-group lessons with up to four students per batch.
Groups are matched by level, confidence, ambition, and regular test performance.

One-to-one support is primarily used for the Diagnostic Bridge where closer assessment is needed before placement.

## Current pricing model

Do not use the previous fixed annual-fee model.
Do not show programme totals.
Do not describe pricing as hourly tuition.
Do not lead with the previous crash-course-first A-Level positioning.

Approved public model:
- KS3 Years 7-8: from £80/month
- Years 9-10 GCSE preparation: from £100/month
- Year 11 GCSE exam year: from £125/month
- AS / Year 12 Maths: £150/month
- Year 13 A-Level Maths: £225/month
