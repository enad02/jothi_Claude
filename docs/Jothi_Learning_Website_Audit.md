# Jothi Learning - Website Audit

Last updated: 1 May 2026

This file supersedes the earlier April audit notes.
The original audit contained useful strategic direction, but its pricing recommendations and A-Level offer positioning are no longer current.

For locked facts and current public decisions, use `SOURCE_OF_TRUTH.md`.
For current implementation status, use `CURRENT_STATE.md`.

## Current audit position

Preserve:
- Static HTML/CSS/JS foundation
- Warm, premium, parent-facing tone
- WhatsApp-first CTA behaviour
- Strong proof packaging through results and verified reviews
- Clear explanation of the Jothi teaching method

Current pricing and programme model:
- Monthly programme-cycle pricing
- No programme totals in public copy
- No hourly-tuition framing
- Stage-based Programmes page decision flow
- Homepage pricing shown only as light `from` monthly prices
- Standard programmes are live small-group lessons with up to four students per batch
- Diagnostic Bridge is the one-to-one entry route where closer assessment is needed before placement

## Current recommended focus

Future audits should focus on:
- Launch readiness and `noindex` removal timing
- Stronger real-world tutor/team credibility assets
- Results proof packaging as more evidence becomes available
- Mobile QA across the shortened Programmes decision flow
- Ensuring public copy stays tool-agnostic and does not drift back into supplier/platform names

## Superseded guidance

Any previous recommendation to use the fixed-fee pricing model, full programme totals, a crash-course-first A-Level offer, or legacy pricing inclusion lists is superseded.
