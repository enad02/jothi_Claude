Years 7–11 Maths Programmes

Structured Maths tutoring from foundation-building to top-grade ambition.

Our Maths programmes support students across Years 7 to 11, from those who need stronger foundations to those aiming for top sets, Grade 8–9 outcomes, or accelerated progress.

This is suitable for families who want:

  • better mathematical understanding
  • stronger problem-solving habits
  • consistent teaching and accountability
  • support that builds over time, not quick fixes

Best for: students who need a clearer system for long-term progress.

GCSE Transformation Programme

For students who are capable, but not yet producing the results they should.

Some students work hard but still produce inconsistent marks. Others understand topics in lessons but cannot apply them properly under test conditions.

The GCSE Transformation Programme is designed to change that.

This programme focuses on:

  • fixing gaps in understanding
  • improving exam technique
  • building stronger routines and consistency
  • turning effort into measurable progress

Best for: students whose results do not yet reflect their potential.

GCSE High-Achiever Pathway

For ambitious students aiming for top grades and stronger mathematical thinking.

This pathway is for students already working at a good level and ready for greater challenge, sharper reasoning, and more demanding preparation.

The goal is not just to keep up. The goal is to move towards Grade 8–9 performance with clarity and discipline.

This pathway includes:

  • higher challenge work
  • deeper reasoning
  • tighter performance tracking
  • preparation aligned to ambitious academic goals

Best for: students aiming for top sets, Grade 8–9, and stronger academic stretch.

10-Month A-Level Maths Crash Course

A fast, demanding pathway for high-ability students ready to move ahead.

This flagship programme is designed for students who are ready to cover the full A-Level Maths specification in 10 months through disciplined teaching and serious commitment.

It is not a casual extension course. It is a high-expectation academic pathway for students who can work quickly and think deeply.

This programme is designed to offer:

  • accelerated progression
  • structured coverage of the full specification
  • close academic guidance
  • a serious stretch beyond the usual school pace

Best for: high-ability students ready for accelerated Maths progression.

Year 9–11 Chemistry and Physics

Structured Science support for students who need clarity, rigour, and exam readiness.

Jothi Learning also supports students in Chemistry and Physics from Year 9 to Year 11, especially those in ambitious school environments who need strong conceptual teaching and regular assessment.

The focus is on:

  • clear topic teaching
  • disciplined recall and retrieval
  • stronger written and calculation-based responses
  • better performance in tests and exams

Best for: students who need structured support in Chemistry or Physics alongside Maths.

UKMT Stretch Pathway

Support for ambitious younger students, including those aiming for UKMT success from Year 7.

For students with strong mathematical curiosity and higher-than-average ability, Jothi offers stretch-focused support designed to deepen thinking and prepare students for richer mathematical challenge.

This pathway supports:

  • stronger reasoning
  • non-routine problem solving
  • mathematical confidence beyond the standard classroom level

Best for: ambitious students who need challenge as well as curriculum progress.
