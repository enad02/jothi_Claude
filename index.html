How Jothi works

Progress starts with knowing what is really blocking it.

We do not add lessons and hope. We first find what is holding your child back, then teach the right content, test understanding, track the evidence, and refine the next step.

The 4T Teaching Engine

Every lesson, topic, and programme follows the same rhythm: teach clearly, test understanding, track the evidence, and transform the next step before small gaps become bigger problems.
